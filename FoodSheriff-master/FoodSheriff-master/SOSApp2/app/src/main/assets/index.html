Food Sheriff SOS


We try to ensure that everyone is able to get food which is a basic need easily.




Our Mission

"To ensure that everyone in the country does not miss a meal especially since it is a basic need."



Our Vision

"A country where everyone has acces to food without the need to struggle getting it in hard ways."



What we do


Announcement

We first of all announce to the public about the food donation collection we want to start so that many people across the country are very aware about the activities that we plan to carry out for the benefit of those people in need.



Locating

We locate the people who have promised to assist us in food donations and sort them according to their counties of residence so that it becomes easier to distribute to the various people in their county who are also in need so that it does not become bulky.



Collection

After locating the places of residence of our donors, we send our trucks to the counties specifically to their homes to go and collect the food donations from them then afterward deliver them to the collection points in each and every county ready for distribution.



Launch

Once all the food items have been brought to the food collection centers in each and every county, we then launch the programme by again sending our trucks to take the food collections to specific people in need in each and every county in under 24 hours of collection.
